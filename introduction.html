Your Comprehensive Guide to Sri Lanka's National Quality Infrastructure for SMEs

Welcome to the QI Catalogue: Your Directory for Quality

This online catalogue serves as an essential **directory and practical guide** for Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs) in Sri Lanka. It is designed to provide clear and concise answers to your most frequently asked questions on quality-related issues, directly linking you to the national quality infrastructure and the institutional framework that supports it.

Whether you're looking to enhance your product quality, ensure compliance with international standards, or navigate complex trade regulations, this resource aims to simplify your journey within Sri Lanka's quality ecosystem.

Key Areas Addressed in this Guide:

  • Quality Management: Covering quality control, quality assurance, and comprehensive management systems.
  • Technical Requirements: Understanding standards, regulations, and measures related to Technical Barriers to Trade (TBT) and Sanitary and Phytosanitary (SPS).
  • Conformity Assessment: Services such as testing, inspection, and certification for product and system compliance.
  • Measurement Science: Insights into metrology services crucial for accuracy and reliability.
  • Competence Recognition: Information on accreditation processes and bodies.
  • Capacity Building: Details on available training and educational programs.

Revision and Edition Information

This publication is a dynamic guide, subject to revision by the Sri Lanka Accreditation Board for Conformity Assessment (SLAB) every two years to ensure its continued relevance and accuracy. The present edition, including all technical details, is based on the version of the catalogue from **October 30, 2018**.

International Cooperation: Strengthening QI in Sri Lanka (PTB)

This initiative is part of an international cooperation project led by the Physikalisch-Technische Bundesanstalt (PTB) – Germany's National Metrology Institute – aimed at bolstering quality infrastructure worldwide.

Project Details:

  • Project Name: Strengthening Quality Infrastructure in Sri Lanka
  • Country Focus: Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
  • Core Objective: To empower small and medium-sized companies from the Northern Region of Sri Lanka by enabling them to make increased use of quality management services that adhere to international good practices.

This collaboration underscores the commitment to fostering a robust and internationally recognized quality landscape in Sri Lanka.
