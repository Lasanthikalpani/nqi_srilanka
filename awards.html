Sri Lanka National Quality Awards

Recognizing excellence in quality and business performance

National Quality Excellence Awards

CNCI Achiever Award for Industrial Excellence

Recognizes outstanding performance and excellence in Sri Lanka's industrial sector.

Organized by: Ceylon National Chamber of Industries (CNCI)

FCCISL Entrepreneur Awards

Honors exceptional entrepreneurs and their contributions to Sri Lanka's economic development.

Organized by: Federation of Chambers of Commerce and Industry of Sri Lanka (FCCISL)

National Awards for Construction Excellence

Acknowledges excellence and innovation in Sri Lanka's construction industry.

Organized by: Construction Industry Development Authority (CIDA)

National Occupational Safety and Health Excellence Awards

Recognizes organizations with outstanding workplace safety and health management systems.

Organized by: Department of Labour (DOL)

National Productivity Awards

Celebrates organizations achieving excellence in productivity enhancement and resource optimization.

Organized by: National Productivity Secretariat (NPS)

NCCSL National Business Excellence Awards

Honors overall business excellence across various sectors of the Sri Lankan economy.

Organized by: National Chamber of Commerce of Sri Lanka (NCCSL)

NCE National Exporter Awards

Recognizes excellence in export performance and contribution to Sri Lanka's export economy.

Organized by: National Chamber of Exporters of Sri Lanka (NCE)

Sri Lanka National Energy Efficiency Awards

Honors organizations demonstrating excellence in energy conservation and efficiency practices.

Organized by: Sri Lanka Sustainable Energy Authority (SLSEA)

Sri Lanka National Quality Award

The highest national recognition for organizational excellence based on international quality frameworks.

Organized by: Sri Lanka Standards Institution (SLSI)

About NQI Sri Lanka

The National Quality Infrastructure (NQI) is the backbone for quality assurance in Sri Lanka, providing the technical framework for standards, metrology, accreditation, and conformity assessment services to enhance the competitiveness of Sri Lankan products and services in global markets.
