Metrology in Sri Lanka

Ensuring accurate measurements for quality, trade, and innovation

National Metrology Services

Metrology is the science of measurement, forming the foundation for quality infrastructure. In Sri Lanka, the Measurement Units, Standards and Services Department (MUSSD) serves as the National Metrology Institute (NMI), ensuring the accuracy and traceability of measurements throughout the country.

Core Metrology Services

  • Scientific Metrology - Development and maintenance of national measurement standards
  • Industrial Metrology - Calibration services for industry instruments
  • Legal Metrology - Regulatory oversight for trade measurements
  • Calibration Services - Ensuring measurement traceability to SI units
  • Metrology Training - Capacity building for industry professionals
  • Measurement Uncertainty Analysis - Technical support for accurate measurements

Scientific Metrology

Maintenance of national measurement standards and dissemination of SI units:

  • Mass and force measurements
  • Dimensional metrology
  • Thermometry and thermal analysis
  • Electrical metrology
  • Time and frequency standards

Industrial Metrology

Calibration services for industrial instruments and equipment:

  • Pressure gauges and transmitters
  • Temperature sensors and indicators
  • Electrical measuring instruments
  • Dimensional measuring tools
  • Torque wrenches and screwdrivers

Legal Metrology

Regulatory functions to ensure fair trade and consumer protection:

  • Type approval of measuring instruments
  • Verification of weighing and measuring devices
  • Market surveillance of prepackaged goods
  • Fuel dispenser accuracy verification
  • Legal metrology enforcement

Importance of Metrology for Industry

  • Quality Assurance - Ensures products meet specifications and quality standards
  • Process Optimization - Improves manufacturing efficiency and reduces waste
  • Regulatory Compliance - Meets national and international requirements
  • Trade Facilitation - Provides measurement credibility for exports
  • Innovation Support - Enables research and development activities
  • Consumer Protection - Ensures fair trade and accurate measurements

About NQI Sri Lanka

The National Quality Infrastructure (NQI) is the backbone for quality assurance in Sri Lanka, providing the technical framework for standards, metrology, accreditation, and conformity assessment services to enhance the competitiveness of Sri Lankan products and services in global markets.
