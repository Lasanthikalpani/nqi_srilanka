Welcome to the National Quality Infrastructure Catalogue for SMEs in Sri Lanka

This website serves as a vital guide for Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs) in Sri Lanka, providing a comprehensive overview of the national quality infrastructure and the essential services offered by its institutional framework. This publication is designed to be a guide for SMEs with answers to their most frequently asked questions on quality-related issues linked to the national quality infrastructure, covering aspects like quality control, quality assurance, technical requirements (standards, regulations, TBT/SPS measures), management systems, conformity assessment services (testing, inspection, and certification), metrology, accreditation, and training.

Our goal is to simplify access to critical information on quality control, assurance, technical requirements, management systems, conformity assessment, metrology, accreditation, and training, empowering SMEs to enhance their competitiveness in both local and international markets.

Explore the various sections to understand the policy frameworks, strategic initiatives, and key institutions dedicated to fostering quality and innovation across Sri Lanka's economic landscape.
