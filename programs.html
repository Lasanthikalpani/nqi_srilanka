National Quality Awards and Programs in Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka National Quality Award (SLNQA)

The Sri Lanka National Quality Award (SLNQA) is the highest honor presented to organizations in Sri Lanka for their excellence in quality management. Administered by the Sri Lanka Standards Institution (SLSI), the award recognizes organizations that demonstrate outstanding performance in various aspects of quality and business excellence, based on a comprehensive set of criteria derived from international quality models.

Eligibility Categories:

The award board has twelve eligibility categories based on the number of full-time employees and the sector:

  • Large manufacturing/service/education/healthcare
  • Medium manufacturing/service/education/healthcare
  • Small manufacturing/service/education/healthcare

In addition to the national quality award, merit awards and commendation certificates are also presented to deserving organizations.

For detailed information on award criteria, application process, and past winners, please refer to the SLSI website.

Other Quality Promotion Programs

Beyond the National Quality Award, various institutions are involved in programs aimed at promoting quality culture, awareness, and capacity building among Sri Lankan industries, especially SMEs.

  • Training Programs: Conducted by SLSI, MUSSD, and SLAB on standards, metrology, conformity assessment, and quality management systems.
  • Awareness Campaigns: Initiatives to educate industries and consumers on the benefits of quality and standards.
  • Technical Assistance: Support provided to SMEs to implement quality management systems and adopt international best practices.
  • Research & Development: Focus on product quality improvement and preservation.

These programs collectively aim to enhance the overall quality infrastructure ecosystem, enabling Sri Lankan businesses to compete effectively in global markets.
