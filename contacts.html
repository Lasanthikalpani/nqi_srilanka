Key Quality Infrastructure Institutions and Contacts in Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka Standards Institution (SLSI)

The National Standards Body of Sri Lanka, responsible for national standards, product certification, management system certification, and metrology services.

  • Address: 17, Victoria Place, Elvitigala Mawatha, Colombo 08, Sri Lanka
  • Tel: +94 011 2694981 (Director Marketing & Promotion)
  • Fax: +94 011 2694981
  • E-mail: dmp@slsi.lk, dharshane@slsi.lk
  • Website: www.slsi.lk
  • Contact Person (Marketing & Promotion): Director (Marketing & Promotion)

Sri Lanka Accreditation Board for Conformity Assessment (SLAB)

The sole national accreditation body for Sri Lanka, accrediting testing, calibration, medical, inspection, and certification bodies.

  • Address: 5th Floor, Sethsiripaya Stage II, Battaramulla, Sri Lanka
  • Tel: +94 11 2887301-3
  • Fax: +94 11 2887304
  • E-mail: info@slab.lk
  • Website: www.slab.gov.lk

Measurement Units, Standards & Services Department (MUSSD)

The National Metrology Institute (NMI) of Sri Lanka, responsible for maintaining national measurement standards and providing calibration services.

  • Address: 11, Galle Road, Colombo 04, Sri Lanka
  • Tel: +94 11 2587399
  • Fax: +94 11 2587399
  • E-mail: mussd@sltnet.lk
  • Website: www.mussd.gov.lk
