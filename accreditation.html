Welcome to the National Quality Infrastructure Catalogue for SMEs in Sri Lanka

This website serves as a vital guide for Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs) in Sri Lanka by providing access to essential quality infrastructure services and resources.

Our goal is to simplify access to critical information on standards, metrology, accreditation, technical regulations, and more to improve SME competitiveness and product quality.

Explore the various sections to understand the policy frameworks, institutional support, and quality-related services available to SMEs.
