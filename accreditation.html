Accreditation in Sri Lanka

Accreditation is the formal recognition that an organization is competent to perform specific tasks. In Sri Lanka, the Sri Lanka Accreditation Board (SLAB) is the national accreditation body that provides internationally recognized accreditation services.

What is Accreditation?

Accreditation is an independent assessment of conformity assessment bodies (CABs) against internationally recognized standards. It provides assurance that:

  • Testing laboratories produce accurate results
  • Certification bodies certify organizations fairly
  • Inspection bodies conduct inspections competently
  • Medical laboratories provide reliable diagnoses
  • Proficiency testing providers offer quality services

Role of SLAB

The Sri Lanka Accreditation Board (SLAB) is the national accreditation body established by the Sri Lanka Accreditation Board Act, No. 32 of 2005.

  • Provides internationally recognized accreditation services
  • Develops accreditation schemes
  • Participates in international mutual recognition arrangements
  • Promotes the value of accreditation nationally
  • Supports national quality infrastructure development

Benefits of Accreditation

  • Enhanced credibility - Internationally recognized proof of competence
  • Market access - Facilitates international trade by reducing technical barriers
  • Risk reduction - Provides assurance of reliable results and certifications
  • Competitive advantage - Differentiates organizations in the marketplace
  • Regulatory acceptance - Recognized by government authorities
  • Improved performance - Promotes continuous improvement

Scope of Accreditation

SLAB offers accreditation for various conformity assessment bodies

Accreditation Type International Standard Examples
Testing & Calibration Labs ISO/IEC 17025 Chemical, mechanical, electrical, biological testing labs
Medical Laboratories ISO 15189 Clinical pathology, microbiology, histopathology labs
Certification Bodies ISO/IEC 17021 Quality, environmental, food safety management systems
Product Certification ISO/IEC 17065 Product certification schemes
Inspection Bodies ISO/IEC 17020 Construction, engineering, commodity inspection
Proficiency Testing ISO/IEC 17043 Providers of proficiency testing schemes

International Recognition

SLAB is a signatory to multilateral recognition arrangements (MRAs) through:

APAC

Asia Pacific Accreditation Cooperation

ILAC

International Laboratory Accreditation Cooperation

IAF

International Accreditation Forum

This recognition means that certificates and reports issued by SLAB-accredited bodies are accepted internationally, facilitating trade and reducing costs for businesses.

Sectoral Accreditation

SLAB provides specialized accreditation for key sectors:

  • Construction - Materials testing, inspection
  • Food & Agriculture - Food testing, certification
  • Textiles - Quality testing, certification
  • Healthcare - Medical labs, diagnostic services
  • Environmental - Water, air, soil testing
  • Energy - Calibration, testing services
